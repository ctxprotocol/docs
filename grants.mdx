## üèÜ Funding Tiers

### What Makes a Tier S Tool?

We don't pay for raw data dumps. We pay for **answers**.

| Feature | Raw Data (Tier B) | Curated Intelligence (Tier S) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Crypto** | "Here's the transaction list" | "Top 5 whales accumulating this token right now" |
| **Finance** | "Here's the SEC filing PDF" | "Key risk factors extracted with sentiment score" |
| **E-commerce** | "Here are Amazon search results" | "Estimated monthly revenue: $47K, trending up 23%" |
| **Competitive Intel** | "Here's their homepage HTML" | "Tech stack: Stripe, Vercel, Segment. 2.3M monthly visitors" |

**Tier S tools do the heavy lifting so the AI can act immediately.**

## üìù How to Apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign in at [ctxprotocol.com](https://ctxprotocol.com) to create your account. This generates your **embedded wallet**‚Äîthe address where you'll receive grants and revenue.
    
    <Tip>
    Copy your signer wallet address from the user nav menu in the bottom left of the main chat app. You'll need it for the application form.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Proposal">
    Fill out our application form with:
    - **Your wallet address** (from Step 1‚Äîrequired for payment)
    - Tool name and description
    - **What expensive service does this replace?** (This helps us understand the value)
    - Which tier you're targeting
    - Technical approach (1-2 paragraphs)
    - Your background (GitHub, portfolio)
    
    üëâ [**Submit Proposal**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfM702YEtiCK5o22bA6hbM7cke7psDbfx4t9-Oj7OsnPjDhtA/viewform)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build & Deploy">
    Once approved, build your MCP server using our SDK:
    
    ```bash
    npm install @ctxprotocol/sdk @modelcontextprotocol/sdk
    ```
    
    **Constraint:** Tools must return data in under 30 seconds. Focus on instant retrieval and analysis.
    
    Follow the [Build Tools Guide](/guides/build-tools) for the complete pattern.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Your Tool">
    Go to [ctxprotocol.com/contribute](https://ctxprotocol.com/contribute) to register your tool:
    - Paste your MCP endpoint URL
    - Set your price per query
    - Add stake to activate your tool
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Paid">
    Once verified, you're live on the marketplace!
    - Agents discover and query your tool
    - You earn 90% of every query fee in USDC
    - Grant payment follows the schedule below
  </Step>
</Steps>
